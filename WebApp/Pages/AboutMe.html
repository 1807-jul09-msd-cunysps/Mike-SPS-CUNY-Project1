About Me

    Hello and thank you for taking the time to get to know me! My name is Michael, I am a software developer with a cybersecurity background and recently graduated from New York University's Master's program for Computer Science. I have loved technology since I was young, Since I can remember I have been taking electronics apart, attempting to figure out what's what, and how to properly put them back together. This aspect of me has followed me down my software development path, always pushing me to better understand the inner-workings of software and how current methods can be improved.

    I have a diverse background related to computer science gained from a combination of real-world practical experience, research projects at NYU, and personal projects I have undertaken to better understand various topics. My previous line of work dealt mostly with compliance related to financial institutions, which transitioned into cyberecurity compliance as the need grew over the last few years. I have worked directly with Bank CISO (Chief Information Security Officers) to assist them in planning and designing their cybersecurity governance to satisfy applicable laws and regulations.

    Since transitioning to a more software-development role, I have gained a broad background in a variety of technologies. I am most comfortable working in back-end development, however, I am also experienced working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have created and consumed various REST APIs using JavaScript, Java, and C#. I have experience working with various databases structures, including SQL and Hadoop. I am comfortable working with Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Azure as cloud providers, and utilizing Git to manage version control.

Thank you again for taking the time to learn a bit more about me!
